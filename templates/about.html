About Us


323 Fairfield Avenue Apartments is a five story luxury hi-rise located at 323 Fairfield Avenue, Bridgeport, CT 06604. We are located in downtown Bridgeport, just two blocks from public transportation! The building is a mixed use property, consisting of 81 residential apartments and 7 retail stores. There is also a secure courtyard in the rear of the building, great for enjoying some fresh air!

Key Features Include:
  • One Bedroom/One Bath Units
    • Appliances included are refrigerator, range, dishwasher, microwave, and washer/dryer
    • Kitchen and living room feature hardwood floors, and bedroom is carpeted
    • Individual central heat and AC
    • Square footage of one bedroom units vary from 804 - 985 square feet
  • Two Bedroom/Two Bath Units
    • Appliances included are refrigerator, range, dishwasher, microwave, and washer/dryer
    • Kitchen and living room feature hardwood floors, and bedroom is carpeted
    • Individual central heat and AC
    • Square footage of one bedroom units vary from 1094 - 1257 square feet

Other Information:
  • Tenants pay for electric (all appliance and heat/AC are electric)
  • Landlord pays for water, sewer and trash removal
  • Rental rates differ based on available units
  • All leases are for one year
  • Pets are welcome in our building
    • We allow one pet per unit with a maximum weight of 20 lbs
    • Pets have a $250 non-refundable pet deposit
  • Parking is available at an extra monthly fee at parking garage located directly across the street
